Sensor accuracy notice

Motion sensor data from your phone provides an estimated lean angle and heading only. Accuracy varies significantly between devices and mounting positions.

Always follow road signs. Speed limits, junction markings, and hazard signs take absolute priority over any app guidance.
Trust your rider sense. RideLine is a guidance aid. If something feels wrong on the road, act on your instincts — not the app.
Phone mounting affects accuracy. Lean angle is estimated from handlebar tilt, not chassis lean. A dedicated IMU sensor gives far better results.
RideLine does not replace training. Always ride within your ability and the limits of the law, road conditions, and your machine.
